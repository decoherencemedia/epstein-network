Epstein Photo Atlas
This is a visualization of images in the Epstein Library. Each circle represents an image and similar images are placed near each other, so clusters usually share a setting, subject, or semantic meaning. Only images containing at least one person we've identified in the network are included.
Circles are colored by the most common category of in-network people appearing in the photo.
  • Hover over a dot (or drag your finger across the map) to preview a photo
  • Click or tap to pin; double-click or tap the thumbnail to open the image in a new tab
  • Scroll or pinch to zoom; drag with one finger on desktop, two fingers on touch devices to pan
Image embeddings were computed using the DINOv2 image model, projected to 2D with the UMAP algorithm, and rendered with D3.js. The scripts are available on GitHub.

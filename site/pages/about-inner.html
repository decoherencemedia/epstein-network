About

This site explores connections between people who appear in photographs from the Epstein Library dataset released by the U.S. Department of Justice.

The Graph view is an interactive network: nodes are people, and edges connect people who appear in the same photograph. Node size reflects how many photos include that person; colors indicate category.

Data processing uses facial recognition (AWS Rekognition) with manual verification, network layout in Gephi, and visualization with D3.js.
